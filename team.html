The manager: Filippo Manavella

Since I was a child I loved creating and designing new things, expecially with legos, and while growing up I became a huge fan of F1 and racing cars in general. So when I saw this project for the first time I immediately decided to take part into it and convince the school to participate in it. Outside F1 my passions are tennis, volleball and playing the piano.

The car engineer and Pilot: Andrea Matullo

I’ve always been passionate about cars and everything related to F1. This project is a unique opportunity for me to get closer to a world I’ve admired from afar, combining my love for motorsport with the chance to challenge myself through teamwork, design, and competition. Being able to contribute to the creation of a real car and watch ideas come to life excites me and motivates me to give my best.

The chief of simulation: Alberto Bruno

I am mainly interested in scientific subjects, especially physics and computer science. Although I had never considered the automotive field as a potential career path, when I came across this project, I saw it as a great opportunity to combine my two passions by working hands-on with a prototype.

The social media manager: Sherine Cirigliano

When I was little, while other girls played with dolls, I played with cars or Lego. One day I saw my father watching F1 on TV so I started watching it too and growing up, it became one of my greatest passions. So, when I discovered about the existence of this project, I immediately wanted to be part of it! In addition to the world of motors, I also have other passions and hobbies such as athletics and music.

The pilot and reporter: Matteo Duse

I have always been a very precise and analytical kid; as I grew up, that part of my character developed into a strong passion for engineering and motorsport. That’s why I chose to join the competition: it was the perfect way to combine my love for design, cars, and a great chance for me to develop some team-work and real-life skills. Until now, thanks to the project I realised this is what I want to pursue in the future.

The graphics designer: Sofia Maragò

My primary passion is scientific subjects, especially maths which I would like to study in the future. My other hobbies are of more creative nature, I like dancing and music, but also designing. When I heard about the project I decided to join in right away because it was something outside of my comfort zone and from which I thought I could learn a lot.
